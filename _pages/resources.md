### Blogs (proteomics):

* [Orsburn Blog - Proteomics News](https://proteomicsnews.blogspot.com/) - check out "Useful Stuff!" sidebar
* **Phil Wilmarth blog/repo** - it is both, but you can read the blog [here](https://pwilmart.github.io/blog/) and navigate the repo [here](https://github.com/pwilmart/Start_Here). There are tutorials, vignettes, and slide decks throughout. For re-analysis I recommend the [LFQ re-analysis of my sea lion urine data](https://github.com/pwilmart/Sea_lion_urine_SpC), and the [re-analysis of Beynon data](https://github.com/pwilmart/Mammalian_sperm_PXD003164).
* **Ron Beavis website** - with all sorts of things ([Main Site](https://www.somecrazyblogger.org/a/index)), specifically his [unofficial dataset annotation](https://www.somecrazyblogger.org/a/tweets?s=pxd)

### Tutorial/Overview Papers:

* [The Anatomy and Taxonomy of Protein Structure by Jane S. Richardson](http://kinemage.biochem.duke.edu/teaching/anatax)
* [An Introduction to Mass Spectrometry-Based Proteomics (Shuken 2023)](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.jproteome.2c00838)
* [Comprehensive Overview of Bottom-Up Proteomics Using Mass Spectrometry (Jiang et al. 2024)](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acsmeasuresciau.3c00068) (or [here](https://jessegmeyerlab.github.io/proteomics-tutorial/) with maybe easier section navigation)
* [A beginner’s guide to mass spectrometry–based proteomics (Sinha and Mann 2020)](https://portlandpress.com/biochemist/article/42/5/64/226371/A-beginner-s-guide-to-mass-spectrometry-based)
* [Emerging mass spectrometry-based proteomics methodologies for novel biomedical applications (Pino 2020)](https://portlandpress.com/biochemsoctrans/article/48/5/1953/226710/Emerging-mass-spectrometry-based-proteomics?searchresult=1)
* [Technical advances in proteomics: new developments in data-independent acquisition (Hu et al, 2016)](https://f1000research.com/articles/5-419)
* [Instrumentation at the Leading Edge of Proteomics (Peters-Clarke et al. 2024)](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.analchem.3c04497)
* [Posterior error probabilities and false discovery rates: two sides of the same coin (Kall 2008)](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/18052118/)
* [Assigning Significance to Peptides Identified by Tandem Mass Spectrometry Using Decoy Databases (Kall 2008)](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/pr700600n)
* [Nature Article: s41580-020-0231-2](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41580-020-0231-2)
